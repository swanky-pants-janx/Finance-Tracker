Your complete personal finance toolkit
1
Finance Planner
Start here. Enter your monthly income, create expense categories, and allocate a budget to each one. This plan becomes the blueprint every budget month is built from — set it once, reuse it forever.
2
Budget Tracker
Each month, open the tracker to log what you actually spend. Fixed expenses like rent get checked off when paid. Variable items like groceries let you log individual transactions and see how close you are to your limit in real time.
3
Savings Planner
Add your savings accounts and set goals. Track how much you've saved, how much is left to reach your target, and project when you'll get there based on your current contribution rate.
4
Interest Calculator
Enter your credit card or loan balances, interest rates, and monthly repayments to see the true cost of your debt — total interest paid, time to pay off, and how extra payments change the picture.
💡 The recommended flow: set up your Finance Plan first, then open the Budget Tracker each month to log spending against it.
