How flanner works
1
Finance Planner
Start here. Enter your income, create expense categories, and allocate funds to each one. This becomes the blueprint every budget month is built from.
2
Budget Tracker
Each month, open the tracker to log what you actually spend. Fixed items get checked off when paid; variable items let you add individual transactions and see how close you are to your limit.
3
Savings Planner
Add your savings accounts and set goals. Track how much you've saved, what's left to reach your target, and when you'll get there at your current pace.
4
Interest Calculator
Enter your credit card or loan balances, interest rates, and monthly repayments to see the true cost of your debt — total interest paid and time to pay it off.
